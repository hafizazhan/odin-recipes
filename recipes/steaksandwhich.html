Steak Sandwhich

The ultimate steak sandwhich is here for you to try! Complete with a tender and juicy steak and sauteed onions served on a toasted hard-roll with garlic-Parmesan mayonnaise, provolone cheese, and Italian seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 4 hard rolls, split
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 pounds round steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced and quartered
  • ½ teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon liquid smoke
  • 1 pinch coarse sea salt
  • 8 (1 ounce) slices provolone cheese
  • ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C). Split rolls open; toast them on a baking sheet in oven while preheating until desired color reached.

  2. Whisk mayonnaise, garlic, and Parmesan cheese together in a small bowl. Refrigerate until ready to use.

  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add steak and onion; season with Worcestershire sauce, liquid smoke, and sea salt. Cook and stir until steak browned and onion tender, about 10 minutes.

  4. Spread garlic-Parmesan mayonnaise generously on both halves toasted rolls. Divide steak and onion mixture among bottom rolls, piling high. Top each with 2 slices provolone cheese; sprinkle with Italian seasoning. Place tops on sandwiches; transfer to a baking sheet.

  5. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese melted, about 5 minutes.
